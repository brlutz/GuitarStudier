How to use jazz chords:

Jazz Chords are amazing, becuase they allow you to instantly play along with many jazz greats. You can start to play along to jazz backing tracks like Autumn Leaves. Not only are you playing Jazz, but you're also learning the fretboard. Knowing where the chords are allows you to use the chord tones when you're soloing over a progression.

Practice Tips:

Like a lot of intermediate guitar pratice tips, there isn't a lot of magic in this, just practice. Find songs you love and learn to apply these chords!

Pick out a popular jazz song, pull up a backing track on Youtube, and go over all the chords you'll need. After some practice, you're ready to play along with the backing track. Don't be afraid to slow down the backing track until you're able to do it full speed. A lot of the fingerings are hard, but with consistant pracice, you'll be playing along in no time, and willhave learned your fretboard!
